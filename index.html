Welcome to Rise of Companion

Calling all Rise of Kingdoms warriors! Presenting Rise of Companion, an innovative tool that integrates state-of-the-art AI technology to help you optimize your gameplay experience. Currently, in its Beta phase, we're committed to making your journey in the game seamless, efficient, and enjoyable.

Current Features:

  • AI-Driven Resource and Speedup Calculation: Forget the hassle of manually calculating your in-game resources and speedups. With Rise of Companion, it's as easy as uploading an image. Our advanced AI instantly identifies and calculates all card items, from food, wood, stone, gold to all speedups. Get all your information at a glance with our clean, user-friendly layout, making resource management effortless and convenient.

Planned Features:

  • Battle Prediction AI: The future of strategic gaming is here. We are developing an AI model trained on countless battle reports to predict battle outcomes with accuracy. This powerful tool will guide you on commander placement, troop count, and more, giving you the edge in every combat scenario.
  • Overlay Button: No more switching between apps or taking screenshots. With our overlay button feature, you'll be able to calculate resources or seek battle assistance on the fly, right from your game screen.
  • Equipment and Crafting Guide: Wondering what to craft next? With our equipment comparison and crafting guide feature, you can craft with confidence, check crafting times, and receive game-play tips designed for your play style and kingdom's age.
